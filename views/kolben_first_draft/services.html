Services

Our Service team has over 100 man years of expertise in repairing and troubleshooting of Piston units. All units coming in for service undergo a thorough check and a feedback is given to the client on techno/commercial points before proceeding with repairs

Mobile Applications

Concrete Pump


We regularly Service Piston Pumps of various brands such as Rexroth – A11VO130 and other models that have been installed in Concrete Pumps such as: Putzmiester – 1407D, Schwing Stetter – SP 8800, SANY – Series 700,1000,1400, Aquarius, Greaves etc…

Excavator


Axial Piston Pumps or Motors such as Kawasaki – K3VL63 and K3VL112T, are serviced on a regular basis installed in Excavators such as: L&T Komatsu – PC-71, PC-210 Avance, PC-300 ; TATA Hitachi – TMX-20, EX 110, EX210 ; KOBELCO – SK220, SK380; Hyundai – R80, R210 ; JCB – JS215LC ; Volvo – EC140D, EC210D; SANY – SY80C, SY210C; DOOSAN – DX63-3, DX235LC etc…

Self Loading Transit Mixture


Our Service Centre excels in repairing Piston Pumps such as Rexroth – A10VG45DA ,A4VG56 DA, A4VG71DA, A6VM80; Bondioli-Pavesi – M4PV50, M4PV32 installed in SLTMs such as: Ajax Fiori – Argo 2000, Argo 4000; Schwing Stetter – SLM 2200, SLM 4000; MACONS – MST-2, MST-4; DMIX – DS 1000, DS2500, DS4000; SNE – SNE200, SNE300 etc…

Knuckle Boom Crane


We service Axial Piston Pumps such as Rexroth – A17F045 Series, Sunfab – SC 047; in Knuckle Boom Cranes in popular makes such as: Cargotec (HIAB) – HIAB X-CL 8, HIAB X-CL 12; Palfinger Cranes – SPK8500, SPK10000,SPK15500 etc…

Road Paver

Tadem Roller

Industrial Applications
