About Us

Kolben Hydraulics Ltd. is a Joint Venture Between Yuken India Ltd. (YIL), one of the pioneers and leading manufacturers of Industrial Hydraulics products in India for the last 40 plus years and other investors. Kolben Hydraulics Ltd is the mobile face of the YIL group of Companies. Strategically, some of the global brands have been brought to India by Kolben. Other complimentary products to increase the basket offerings is on going.

PWG srl, Italy


Focused on Hydrostatic Pumps and Motors from 14 cc to 64 cc rated up to 400 bar, single and tandem pumps, filters, joysticks, valve blocks, Hydraulic-driven generators, compressors and welding unit. A Joint Venture Company is formed and localizing of parts in a phased manner is in progress.

PSM Hydraulics, Russia


Swash plate closed loop pumps/ motors, bent axis designed fixed /variable pumps and motors rated up to 450 bar, joysticks, slew drive, track/ wheel drives, Splitter gearbox with integrated pumps. Range of products starts from 12 cc up to 250 cc .Many products are analogous to other brands like Bosch Rexroth, Sauer Danfoss, Linde, Parker , Eaton, Dana – Brevini etc. To meet the needs of our customers, a Joint venture agreement is signed and localizing of parts in a phased manner is in progress.

Sunfab, Sweden


Bent axis fixed displacement pumps/ motors ranging from 12 cc to 130 cc with options of mounting/ shaft rated upto 450 bar peak. Cartridge Motors, High pressure Variable displacement pumps, PTO drives, add on valve block etc.

Tecnord – Delta, Italy/USA


High end mobile proportional valves , Mechanical, On/Off and proportional cartridge valves, Electronics joystick, Electronic cards and specific machine management system

Kolben's Package solutions for Mobile OEM’s


With a wide range of products handled and also access to other group products including electronics, Kolben’s application team provides system solutions for a wide range of machines like – Harvestors, Self-Loading Transit mixer, Skid Steer Loader, Sweeping machines, Walk Behind Rollers etc.

Service Solutions


Kolben is one the first Company in India to start an “ All brand Hydraulics service center across PAN India” in the organized sector. At this point two service centers are operational – one in Bangalore, which is also our Head office and a second center in Delhi. Plans are to have more service centers across India at strategic locations. In our Service center, apart from the brands represented, other popular brands like Bosch Rexroth, Sauer Danfoss, Parker, Eaton , Kawasaki etc. are serviced. Kolben is looking to rapidly expand both geographically in India and abroad in the coming years.
